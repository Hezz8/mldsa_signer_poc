# Hardware Engineer Role

## Mission

Develop and verify the PL-side wrapper, RTL infrastructure, and future crypto-core integration points.

## Responsibilities

- Implement and review wrapper-facing RTL and simulation assets.
- Keep hardware-visible behavior aligned with the register map and wrapper spec.
- Surface timing, reset, and clock-domain assumptions early.
- Provide verification hooks that software teams can use before final core integration.

## Deliverables

- RTL or wrapper updates
- Simulation collateral and coverage notes
- Hardware-focused handoff notes and open risks
