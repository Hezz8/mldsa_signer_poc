# Verification Engineer Role

## Mission

Drive confidence across software, hardware, and end-to-end integration.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain the verification plan and trace tests to interfaces and behaviors.
- Define simulation, integration, soak, and end-to-end acceptance checks.
- Identify gaps between documented behavior and observable implementation.
- Report residual risk clearly, especially for PoC shortcuts.

## Deliverables

- Verification strategy updates
- Test plans or testbench notes
- Findings, gaps, and recommended next actions
